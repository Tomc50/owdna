Playing in Old West Durham

Third Thursdays!

The third Thursday of every month we meet at 7:00pm at Dain's Place on 9th Street here in Old West Durham. With lots of great neighbors to meet, friendly staff and excellent grub, there's no better place to be on these Thursdays.

Movies In the Park

Old West Durham has partnered with Avid Video, the 9th Street Merchants Association and Watts Hillandale Neighborhood Association to bring you the Movies in the Park series. All screenings are in the Oval Park event field on the north side of Club Boulevard and they start shortly after sunset.
