Playing in Old West Durham

Third Thursdays!

The third Thursday of every month we meet at 7:00pm at Dain's Place on 9th Street here in Old West Durham. With free beer for the first newcomer to arrive, lots of great neighbors to meet, friendly staff and excellent grub, there's no better place to be on these Thursdays.
