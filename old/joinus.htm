Become a Member of OWDNA!

It's your neighborhood and you can make a difference.

 

The Old West Durham neighborhood stretches from Broad Street west to Hillandale Road and from Englewood Avenue south to the Durham Freeway.


The Old West Durham Neighborhood Association board meets the last Thursday of every month at 7pm. For more information, please contact Catherine Hart (President).
