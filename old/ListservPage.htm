Neighborhood listserve

Join the OWDNA Yahoo Group to stay in the loop about neighborhood issues.

To subscribe to the Old West Durham listserve, send a blank message to:
owdna-subscribe@yahoogroups.com

Once you've joined, please read and observe our etiquette guidelines. Then you can:

  • Post a message by sending it to: owdna@yahoogroups.com

  • Catch up on what's been said before by browsing the listserve archive:
    groups.yahoo.com/group/owdna

  • And, if you must, unsubscribe by sending a blank message to:
    owdna-unsubscribe@yahoogroups.com
