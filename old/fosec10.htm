Storm Drain Stickers

Duke Youth Program volunteers work with Durham Stormwater Services to place 87 "Don't Pollute" stickers on high-visibility storm drains along South Ellerbe Creek in Durham Central Park, Historic Durham Athletic Park, and Walltown. Volunteers also distributed 200 education flyers about how to make our city creeks healthier. (Photographs are courtesy of Durham Stormwater Services.)
